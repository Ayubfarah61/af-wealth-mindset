14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All products — no questions asked.

If you are not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 14 days of purchase and we will issue a full refund. Payments are processed by Paddle.

At AF Wealth Mindset, we are confident in the value of our products and want you to feel completely safe making a purchase. We offer a straightforward, hassle-free refund policy on all digital products.

1. Refund Window

  • All digital products: 14-day money-back guarantee from the date of purchase.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, simply send an email to sales@afwealthmindset.com with the following information:

  • Your full name or the email address used at checkout
  • The product you purchased
  • Your transaction or order reference number (found in your confirmation email)
  • The reason for your refund request (optional — but helpful for us to improve)

You do not need to provide a reason to receive your refund. We honour refund requests without question within the guarantee period.

3. Processing Time

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will confirm receipt of your request within 1 business day.
  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
  • The refunded amount will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout. All payments are processed by Paddle.com — refunds will be returned through the same Paddle transaction.

4. After a Refund

Upon processing a refund, we kindly request that you:

  • Delete or destroy any downloaded copies of the product
  • Discontinue use of the product and any associated materials

Continued use of materials after a refund may be considered a breach of our Terms of Service.

5. Affiliate Products

Some products on this website are offered through affiliate partnerships (e.g., Cashflow Secrets via Money Ripples). In this case, the refund is handled directly by the product creator or their platform. By clicking through to their purchase page, you agree to their refund terms, which are comparable to or more generous than ours. We will assist you in facilitating any such refund request.

6. Chargebacks

We ask that you contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. We are committed to resolving any issue promptly and a direct refund is usually much faster than a chargeback process. Fraudulent chargebacks may result in being banned from future purchases.

7. Contact

For refund requests or any questions about this policy:
Email: sales@afwealthmindset.com
We aim to respond to all emails within 1 business day.
