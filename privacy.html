Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

AF Wealth Mindset ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you visit afwealthmindset.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email address — provided by you at checkout or when subscribing to our newsletter, used to deliver your purchase and send relevant communications.
  • Payment information — processed entirely by our payment providers (PayPal and Paystack). We never see or store your card number, CVV, or banking credentials.
  • Transaction reference — a unique identifier stored locally on your device to track your orders within the app.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as pages visited, time on site, and browser type, collected automatically to improve our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver your digital product purchase to your email address
  • Send order confirmation and transaction receipts
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Send newsletter and promotional emails (only if you subscribed — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve the functionality and content of our website
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may process your data:

  • Paddle — authorised reseller and payment processor. Paddle handles all payment data and is PCI-DSS compliant. Paddle Privacy Policy
  • EmailJS — used to send order confirmation emails. Your email address is transmitted to deliver your purchase notification.
  • Cloudflare — hosting infrastructure and security. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing the data they collect and process.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store:

  • Your order history (product name, price, payment method, transaction ID)
  • Your account preferences (if you create a profile)

This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-party services we use (such as PayPal and Cloudflare) may set their own cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as necessary to fulfil your purchase, provide customer support, and comply with legal obligations. If you subscribed to our newsletter, we retain your email until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data for marketing purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@afwealthmindset.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. All payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant providers. Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
